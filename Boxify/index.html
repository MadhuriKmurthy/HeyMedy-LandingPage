Services

Medy Search

An advanced search platform will help you find medical facilities like Hospitals, Clinics & Pharmacies. Our system help you identify a particular specialty/specialist based on the physical symptoms, thus cutting down the process of getting a general assessment.

Know your Hosptial

Our system will help you understand the hospitals and clinics by giving sufficient information about them before you decide whom to visit for your health related needs.

Medy Wallet

Our unique inbuilt wallet helps you manage your money in a smart way and use it for availing medical facilities, buy medicines etc. This is an escrow account where you deposit a smaller portion of money and let it grow and use it during the medical requirements..




Medy Review

User can review medical facilities based on their experiences with them, give them feedback. This is an open platform where other users view such reviews and the culminated review will help them make smart choices.

Medy Tips

Our Health Tips section will have feeds from trusted sources; we have a team of medical experts verify every feed we showcase.

Medy Forum

A forum where users get to discuss with the medical experts about range of medical topics, posts questions and get answers by the experts..

Medy Coupons

Users can buy/avail ‘Medy Coupons’, on our platform. They can redeem these coupons for their treatments/consultations/medicine purchases etc.
